Privacy Policy for PetDNS

Last updated: May 9, 2026

PetDNS ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy describes how the App handles information.

Data Collection

PetDNS does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. The App does not include analytics libraries, advertising SDKs, or any third-party services that have access to your information.

API Key Storage

To use PetDNS, you provide your NextDNS API key. This key is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's standard on-device secure storage. It is never transmitted to any server operated by the developer.

Data Displayed in the App

PetDNS connects directly from your device to the NextDNS API to download and display your account data (such as query logs and settings). This data is fetched on demand and is not stored by the developer. The developer has no visibility into your NextDNS account data.

NextDNS

PetDNS is a third-party application and is not affiliated with or endorsed by NextDNS. For information about how NextDNS handles your data, please review the NextDNS Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised "Last updated" date.
